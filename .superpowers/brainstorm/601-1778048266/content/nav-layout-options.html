How to handle 10 nav items

The top pill nav can comfortably hold ~5 items. Here are 3 patterns for fitting all 10.

Sidebar pill (on scroll)
All 10 items
Top pill: Home, About, Projects, Contact, Resume

Sidebar pill (scroll): All 10 grouped by type

A — Split: 5 in pill, all 10 in sidebar

Top pill shows the 5 most-visited items. Sidebar on scroll shows everything including Skills, Experience, Education, Services, GitHub. Clean at both states.

All 10 as icons — hover shows tooltip
Same pill used on top AND as sidebar when scrolled

B — Icon-only pill, all 10 items

The pill shows all 10 as icons with tooltips on hover. On scroll, the same set moves to the left sidebar. One unified nav — no split. Compact but requires users to learn the icons.

C — Two-tier: utility links top-right, section links in pill

GitHub, Resume, Contact stay as action buttons in the top-right. The pill below holds all 7 section links (Home → Services). Sidebar on scroll mirrors this split.

My recommendation: A — keeps the top pill focused on what visitors actually want to jump to, and the sidebar handles everything when reading.
